AurumFold Rural NT EOI Landing Page

Files:
- index.html (open this in your browser)
- assets/ (images + logo files)

To receive EOI submissions:
Option A (recommended): Use a form endpoint like Formspree/Getform
- Open index.html
- Find: data-endpoint=""
- Paste your endpoint URL inside the quotes

Option B (simple): Email fallback
- Open index.html
- Find: you@example.com
- Replace with your email
